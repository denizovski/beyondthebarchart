First, I would like to emphasize the importance of this study. Your participation will help us gain valuable insights into how people interact with data visualization, which can inform future design and development. This research has the potential to make a significant impact in the field and contribute to a better understanding of how data can be effectively communicated to a wide range of audiences.

Second, I would like to reassure you that confidentiality and privacy are of utmost importance to us. The data collected during the study will be kept confidential and will be stored securely. We will also be taking measures to de-identify the data to ensure that your personal information is not disclosed.

Additionally, we believe that your participation will provide you with an opportunity to reflect on your own perceptions and thoughts about data visualization. Although there may not be any direct benefits to you for participating in this study, you will have the satisfaction of knowing that you are contributing to important research.

Finally, I would like to emphasize that participation in this study is completely voluntary. If you have any concerns or if at any point you feel uncomfortable, you are free to withdraw from the study without any negative consequences.

Thank you again for considering participation in this study. If you have any further questions or concerns, please do not hesitate to contact me.

Sincerely,

Deniz Tural
