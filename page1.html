Informed Consent Form for Observational Study with Video and Audio Recording 


------
The participants read, understood, and accepted the following information regarding privacy and ethical considerations before participating in the study. Please take a moment to review the information before proceeding.
------


You are being asked to participate in a research study conducted Deniz Tural as part of final thesis of Digital Humanities and Digital Knowledge Master Degree. The purpose of this study is to examine how people interact with data visualization.

If you agree to participate in this study, you will be asked to view a data visualization and answer specific questions then share your thoughts and opinions while viewing it. The session will be recorded using video and audio. The recording will be used for analysis purposes only and will be kept confidential.

The session is expected to take approximately 15-20 minutes.

There are no direct benefits to you for participating in this study. However, your participation will contribute to a better understanding of how people interact with data visualization and the importance of using the right data visualization tools.
There are no known risks associated with this study. However, if you feel uncomfortable during the recording, you may discontinue your participation at any time.

All data collected during the study will be kept confidential. The recordings will be stored securely and will only be accessible by the researchers.

Your participation in this study is voluntary. You may choose not to participate or withdraw from the study at any time without any negative consequences.

If you have any questions about the study, please contact with me; deniz.tural@studio.unibo.it



I have read and understand the information provided in this informed consent form. I voluntarily agree to participate in this study.
